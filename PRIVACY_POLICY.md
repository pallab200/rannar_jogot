# Privacy Policy for Rannar Jogot

**Last Updated:** May 26, 2026

## 1. Overview

Rannar Jogot ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly
- **Favorites:** When you mark videos as favorites, this data is stored locally on your device.
- **Preferences:** Your playback preferences (autoplay, theme, language) are stored locally using SharedPreferences.

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Video Viewing Data:** YouTube video IDs and playback events are transmitted to YouTube (via the YouTube Player iframe) to enable video playback and analytics.
- **Advertising Data:** Google Mobile Ads may collect limited device identifiers and usage patterns to serve personalized ads. See Google's privacy policy for details: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- **Crash/Performance Data:** We may collect basic app performance and crash logs to improve stability.

### 2.3 Third-Party Data Collection
- **YouTube:** Video metadata, thumbnails, and playback data are retrieved from YouTube API. See YouTube's privacy policy: https://www.youtube.com/t/privacy
- **Google Mobile Ads:** Ad-serving and analytics. See Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- **Google Analytics (if enabled):** May collect usage analytics. See Google Analytics privacy: https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/6004245

## 3. How We Use Your Information

- **To provide services:** Display cooking videos, enable playback, and manage your favorites
- **To improve the App:** Understand how users interact with the App and fix bugs
- **To serve ads:** Google Mobile Ads may use data for personalized advertising
- **To comply with law:** If legally required

## 4. Data Storage & Retention

- **Local Data:** Favorites and preferences are stored only on your device and are never sent to our servers.
- **Remote Data:** YouTube video data is retained by YouTube per their policies.
- **Advertising Data:** Retained by Google per their retention policies.

## 5. Data Sharing

We **do not sell or share** your personal data with third parties, except:
- **YouTube:** Video IDs and basic playback data (necessary for video playback)
- **Google Mobile Ads:** Basic analytics for ad serving
- **Legal requirements:** If compelled by law

## 6. Your Rights & Controls

- **Local Data:** You can clear all locally stored data (favorites, preferences) by uninstalling the App or clearing app data in your device settings.
- **Ad Personalization:** You can opt out of personalized ads in your device's Google Ads Settings: https://adssettings.google.com
- **YouTube Account:** If you are signed into YouTube, you can manage your watch history and privacy settings at https://myactivity.google.com

## 7. Children Under 13

The App may display personalized ads and collect limited usage data as described above. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information without your consent, please contact us immediately.

**Note:** We comply with Google Play's Families Policy and do not knowingly collect sensitive personal information from children.

## 8. Data Security

We employ reasonable technical measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. You use the App at your own risk.

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
