# Rannar Jogot — Data Deletion Request

**Last Updated:** May 26, 2026

## How to Delete Your Data

Rannar Jogot stores user data locally on your device. Follow the steps below to request deletion of all app-related data.

---

## Local Data Deletion (Immediate)

### On Android:

1. **Open Settings** → **Apps** (or **Application Manager**)
2. **Find "Rannar Jogot"**
3. Tap **Storage** (or **Storage and Cache**)
4. Select **Clear Storage** (or **Clear Data**)
   - This removes all local data: favorites, watch history, preferences
5. **Confirm** the action

**Alternative:** Uninstall the app completely:
1. **Settings** → **Apps** → **Rannar Jogot**
2. Tap **Uninstall**
3. All app data will be deleted

---

## Data Deleted

When you clear local app data or uninstall, the following is deleted:

- ✅ Favorites list
- ✅ Watch history
- ✅ Playback preferences (autoplay, theme)
- ✅ Cached videos and thumbnails
- ✅ App-generated logs (if any)

---

## Data NOT Deleted (Third-Party Retention)

The following data is **not** managed by Rannar Jogot and persists after deletion:

- **YouTube:** Video IDs you watched, watch history (managed by YouTube)
  - Manage at: https://myactivity.google.com
- **Google Mobile Ads:** Ad ID and personalization data (managed by Google)
  - Manage at: https://adssettings.google.com
- **Server Logs:** Anonymous crash/performance logs (deleted within 90 days per Google's retention policy)

## Data Retention Policy

- **Local data:** Retained until user manually deletes or uninstalls
- **YouTube data:** Managed by YouTube; see https://www.youtube.com/t/privacy
- **AdMob data:** Managed by Google; see https://policies.google.com/privacy
- **Server logs:** Deleted within 90 days
